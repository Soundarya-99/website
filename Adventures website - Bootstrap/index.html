Hiking the Italian Alps

Hike one of the most beautiful mountain landscapes in the world. Italy’s dramatic rocky rooftop, the Dolomites offer spectacular views and lovely surroundings, especially on this spring tour. You will enjoy the wild Alpine meadows, snow-tipped peaks and cobblestone mountain villages. We will take scenic paths from Cortina d’ Ampezzo to Alta Badia and learn about the fascinating history of the region along the way.

Cycling from Bangkok to Chiang Mai

Discover the charm of the Thai countryside, on this ten-day cycling tour from Bangkok to Chiang Mai. Along the way, we will visit Buddhist Temples tucked into the cool and lush rainforests, ride down the Chao Phraya River on long-tailed boat and spend the Chiang Mai Elephant Conservatory. This trip an Adventure Company favorite among families.

Rock Climbing in the Czech Republic

This two-day tour begins with a stay at the ancient Karlštejn Castle established by Roman Emperor Charles IV to store crowned jewels and holy relics. After our castle stay, we will move on to Cesky Raj, whose translates into Bohemian Paradise, the oldest nature reserve in the Czech Republic.
